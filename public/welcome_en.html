The app works like a native app — it opens fullscreen with no browser bar, and lives on your phone's home screen just like any other app.

Installation Instructions
🍎

iPhone / iPad (iOS)

Use Safari browser

1
Open Safari (not Chrome or another browser)
2
Go to:
unorthodox-athletes.vercel.app
3
Tap the Share button □↑ at the bottom center of the screen
4
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
5
Tap "Add" in the top right — done! ✓
🤖

Android

Use Chrome browser

1
Open Chrome
2
Go to:
unorthodox-athletes.vercel.app
3
Tap the 3-dot menu ⋮ in the top right
4
Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install App"
5
Tap "Add" — done! ✓
Notifications
🔔

Stay in the loop

What you'll receive notifications about

Session booked — Confirmation when your trainer logs a new session for you

Session cancelled — Immediate alert if a session is cancelled

Slot opened up — Get notified when a previously full slot becomes available

Announcements — Important updates from your trainer (schedule changes, closures, etc.)

Package reminders — Heads up when your sessions are running low or a payment is pending

Tip: Notifications appear inside the app (bell icon). For push notifications on your phone, allow notifications when prompted after installing the app on your home screen.
